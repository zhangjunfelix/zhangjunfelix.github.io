Pre-registration

Preregistration Preregistration is the practice of documenting your research plan at the beginning of your study and storing that plan in a read-only public repository, such as OSF Registries or As Predicted.org. Benefits of Preregistration Clarity: Ensures that research teams share a clear understanding of their research goals and processes. Transparency: Separates the confirmatory aspects of the research from the exploratory aspects. Collaboration: Allows for input from colleagues online prior to conducting the study. Pre-acceptance: Enables journal editors to accept researchersâ€™ papers conditionally, regardless of the outcomes.
